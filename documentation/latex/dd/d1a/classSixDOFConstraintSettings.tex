{\footnotesize\ttfamily float Six\+DOFConstraint\+Settings\+::m\+Limit\+Min\mbox{[}EAxis\+::\+Num\mbox{]} = \{ -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX, -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX, -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX, -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX, -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX, -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX \}}

Limits. For translation\+: Min and max linear limits in m (0 is frame of body 1 and 2 coincide). For rotation\+: Min and max angular limits in rad (0 is frame of body 1 and 2 coincide). See comments at Axis enum for limit ranges.

Remove degree of freedom by setting min = FLT\+\_\+\+MAX and max = -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX. The constraint will be driven to 0 for this axis.

Free movement over an axis is allowed when min = -\/FLT\+\_\+\+MAX and max = FLT\+\_\+\+MAX.

Rotation limit around X-\/\+Axis\+: When limited, should be $\in [-\pi, \pi]$. Can be asymmetric around zero.

Rotation limit around Y-\/Z Axis\+: Forms a pyramid or cone shaped limit\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item For pyramid, should be $\in [-\pi, \pi]$ and does not need to be symmetrical around zero.
\item For cone should be $\in [0, \pi]$ and needs to be symmetrical around zero (min limit is assumed to be -\/max limit). 
\end{DoxyItemize}\Hypertarget{classSixDOFConstraintSettings_a43f8dc31c115124722b98689fce631f7}\label{classSixDOFConstraintSettings_a43f8dc31c115124722b98689fce631f7}
