\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Store vertices in 64 bits and indices in 8 bits + 8 bit of flags per triangle like this\+:

\doxylink{structTriangleCodecIndexed8BitPackSOA4Flags_1_1TriangleBlockHeader}{Triangle\+Block\+Header}, \doxylink{structTriangleCodecIndexed8BitPackSOA4Flags_1_1TriangleBlock}{Triangle\+Block} (4 triangles and their flags in 16 bytes), \doxylink{structTriangleCodecIndexed8BitPackSOA4Flags_1_1TriangleBlock}{Triangle\+Block}...

Vertices are stored\+:

\doxylink{structTriangleCodecIndexed8BitPackSOA4Flags_1_1VertexData}{Vertex\+Data} (1 vertex in 64 bits), \doxylink{structTriangleCodecIndexed8BitPackSOA4Flags_1_1VertexData}{Vertex\+Data}...

They\textquotesingle{}re compressed relative to the bounding box as provided by the node codec.
